Database files are as follows:

combined_LSU_v2.fasta
rRNA LSU sequences extracted from the NCBI prokaryote and eukaryote LSU BLAST databases dated 26 April 2024 after removing a single accession contaminated with SSU rRNA.

General_EUK_SSU_v2.0_cleaned_final2.SSU.fasta
General_EUK_SSU_v2.0_cleaned_final2.SSU.tax
18S sequences from the Eukaryome SSU database after removal of prokaryotes, mitochondria and plastids; truncating at 3’ end using ITSx and removing any LSU contamination.
Tedersoo, L., Hosseyni Moghaddam, M.S., Mikryukov, V., Hakimzadeh, A., Bahram, M., Nilsson, R.H., Yatsiuk, I., Geisen, S., Schwelm, A., Piwosz, K. et al. (2024) EUKARYOME: the rRNA gene reference database for identification of all eukaryotes. Database, 2024.

KSGP_plus2.tax
KSGP_v4.0.fasta
Version 4.0 of the KSGP database containing Eukaryote SSU sequences derived from Eukaryome v2.0 (as described above)

SLV_138.1_SSU_cleaned.fasta
Version 138.1 of the SILVA SSU database after removing LSU contamination
