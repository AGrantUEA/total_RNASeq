These are the database files required to run the total_RNASeq script

sortme_plus_ncbi_ssu.fasta is the recommended database to use with SortMeRNA - this consists of the SortMeRNA sensitive database version 4.3 with LSU contamination removed (see Kopylova, https://sortmerna.readthedocs.io/en/latest/) and eukaryote and prokaryote SSU databases obtained from NCBI. The NCBI SSU data are derived from the NCBI Eukaryote BLAST database dated 26 April 2024 concatenated with Bacteria and Archaea 16S sequences from the REFSEQ targeted loci project dated 21 February 2025. See Grant and Davies, 2026 for more details.

Kopylova, E., Noe, L. and Touzet, H. (2012) SortMeRNA: fast and accurate filtering of ribosomal RNAs in metatranscriptomic data. Bioinformatics, 28, 3211-3217.
National Center for Biotechnology Information. (2002) 16S RefSeq records processing and curation https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/refseq/targetedloci/16S_process/
National Center for Biotechnology Information. (2013) NCBI RefSeq Targeted Loci Project https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/refseq/targetedloci/
National Center for Biotechnology Information. (2026) Index of /blast/db https://ftp.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/blast/db/
